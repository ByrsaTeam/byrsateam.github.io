"What if a financial app could be as powerful as a bank, but as private as your personal diary?"

Most personal finance apps force you to create an account, sync your bank credentials to a cloud, and quietly monetize your spending data. BudgetZen was built around the opposite premise: absolute privacy. No account. No cloud. No tracking. It works entirely offline, on your device.
